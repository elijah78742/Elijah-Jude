Welcome to New Life Comprehensive College

A premier co-educational secondary school delivering holistic cognitive, moral, and vocational excellence in Oyo State.

Distraction-Free Campus

Our expansive Soka Campground facility provides clean air, top-tier security, modern athletic courts, and quiet surroundings conducive to intensive academic study.

Dual-Curriculum Mastery

We blend standard Nigerian NERDC syllabus requirements with international STEM best practices, preparing students for BECE, WASSCE, NECO, JAMB, and Cambridge checkpoints.

Pastoral & Moral Mentorship

Every student is paired with a faculty advisor who tracks character traits, discipline, emotional wellness, and daily punctuality.

Academic Schools & Departments

Comprehensive subject pathways tailored to individual strengths and future career ambitions.

Junior Secondary (JSS 1-3)

Focuses on foundational numeracy, literacy, Basic Science & Technology, Pre-vocational studies, and National Values Education.

Senior Science & Tech

Arts, Humanities & Commerce

Preparatory tracks in Literature-in-English, Government, Financial Accounting, Economics, and Business Management.
